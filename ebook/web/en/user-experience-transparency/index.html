📋 Movement Overview

The third movement focuses on user experience and system transparency. How to make AI comprehensible, usable and trustworthy for all stakeholders.

💬 Conversational Interface

Context-aware chat that understands the current project. Conversational interfaces that integrate seamlessly into workflows.

🔍 Deep Reasoning

Open the AI black box. Show thinking, confidence levels, and decision process transparency to build trust.

🎨 Minimal UX Design

Essential interface Claude/ChatGPT style. Focus on content, eliminating distractions to maximize productivity.

🧪 Testing & Validation

B2B and Fitness testing to demonstrate versatility. Cross-domain validation and realistic stress testing.

🎯 What You'll Learn

  • Design conversational interfaces that integrate seamlessly into workflows without interruptions
  • Implement AI transparency with show_thinking and confidence levels for building trust
  • Minimal UX design patterns that maximize productivity by eliminating distractions
  • Cross-domain testing strategies to demonstrate versatility and robustness
  • Chain-of-thought reasoning for quality assurance and decision transparency
  • Onboarding patterns that guide users toward successful adoption
